Homemade Pizza

Description

A simple, crispy homemade pizza that you can top with whatever you have in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Stretch out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  2. Spread an even layer of sauce over the dough.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese and add pepperoni slices.
  4. Bake at 450°F for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden.
