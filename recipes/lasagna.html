Classic Lasagna

Description

This is a classic, hearty lasagna recipe packed with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet.
  2. Mix the ricotta and mozzarella cheeses together.
  3. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes until bubbly.
