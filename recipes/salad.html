Caesar Salad

Description

A crisp, refreshing Caesar salad with homemade garlic croutons and a tangy dressing.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing in a large bowl.
  3. Top generously with parmesan cheese and croutons.
  4. Serve immediately.
